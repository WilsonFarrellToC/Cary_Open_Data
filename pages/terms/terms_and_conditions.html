Under state law known as the "Public Records Law" ( NCGS 132-1 et.seq), the documents, papers, letters, maps, books, photographs, films, sound recordings, magnetic or other tapes, electronic data-processing records, artifacts, or other documentary material made or received by the Town in the transaction of public business are public records which, for the most part, are open to public inspection and copying. To facilitate the public inspection of public records, the Town of Cary makes certain records and data available on this Town of Cary Open Data Portal. As a courtesy to, and for the benefit of, the public, the Town requires that those who utilize these records attribute that content to the Town of Cary, North Carolina. All records and data published on the Town of Cary Open Data Portal is published under the Open Database License (ODbL) v1.0 license ('License'), the full text of which can be found at this link and which is herein incorporated by reference. By accessing and/or utilizing this Open Data Portal, you agree to be bound by the terms of service listed on this page as well as the terms of the License.

The Town's Web Site and the Open Data Portal collect information, including email addresses, of users. Most, if not all, of these records and information is a public record, as are the email addresses of those who subscribe to the web site's electronic mailing lists and the user profile data routinely collected by the Town from visitors to the Web Site and Open Data portal.

Visitor information routinely captured includes the:

  • Internet domain, IP address, state and/or country from which visitors reach the site;
  • Date and time
  • Pages visited.

The Town of Cary uses these records and information to make the web site and Open Data portal more useful to visitors. The Town abides by the Public Records Law and other laws, and while these records are public records, the Town does not sell or otherwise profit from any public records or information obtained from or on the Web Site or Open Data Portal.
